Salmon and rice for lunch break

  • Salmon
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Microwave rice
  • Oven safe glass food containers

Step-by-step guide

  1. Place salmon in the food containers
  2. Season salmon
  3. Place salmon in the oven at 180 degrees for 25 minutes
  4. Remove containers from the oven and allow them to cool down
  5. Place containers in the fridge
  6. During lunch break microwave salmon and rice for 2 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
