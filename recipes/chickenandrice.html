Chicken and rice for lunch break

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Microwave rice
  • Oven safe glass food containers

Step-by-step guide

  1. Place chicken in the food containers
  2. Season chicken
  3. Place chicken in the oven at 180 degrees for 45-50 minutes
  4. Remove containers from the oven and allow them to cool down
  5. Place containers in the fridge
  6. During lunch break microwave chicken and rice for 2 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
